# Email Assistant Skill

This skill provides NexusClaw agents with intelligent email management capabilities.

## Usage
Agents can utilize this skill to:
- Draft professional emails in various tones
- Summarize long email threads
- Schedule emails for optimal send times
- Auto-reply to common queries
- Organize inbox with smart filters

## Examples
- "Draft a professional email to my client about the project delay"
- "Summarize this email thread and tell me what I need to do"
- "Create an auto-reply for when I'm on vacation"
- "Organize my inbox and prioritize urgent emails"

## Prompts
The skill uses specialized prompts for different email tasks:
- **Draft Email**: Creates professional emails with specified tone and length
- **Summarize Thread**: Extracts key points and action items
- **Auto Reply**: Generates contextual automatic responses
- **Organize**: Categorizes and prioritizes emails intelligently
