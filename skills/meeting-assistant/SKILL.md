# Meeting Assistant Skill

This skill provides NexusClaw agents with comprehensive meeting management capabilities.

## Usage
Agents can utilize this skill to:
- Schedule and coordinate meetings across time zones
- Generate professional meeting agendas
- Take structured meeting notes in real-time
- Extract and track action items
- Send follow-up emails with summaries

## Examples
- "Schedule a meeting with the team next week to discuss Q2 goals"
- "Generate an agenda for our product review meeting"
- "Summarize these meeting notes and extract action items"
- "Draft a follow-up email for today's client meeting"

## Capabilities
- Smart scheduling with conflict detection
- Time zone coordination
- Agenda templates for different meeting types
- Real-time note-taking assistance
- Action item tracking with assignments
- Automated follow-up generation
